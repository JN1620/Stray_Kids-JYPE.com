STRAY KIDS

   Stray Kids (스트레이 키즈) is a South Korean boy group under JYP Entertainment. The group consists 

of 8 members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. Stray Kids 

debuted on March 25, 2018, after being formed through a survival program of the same name.

Stray Kids Fandom Name: "Stay"

   The group's name Stray Kids was not defined by the agency JYP Entertainment but was designed by 

the members themselves. It was originally intended to speak of a lost child who wants to chase 

their dreams and then evolved into a sort of idea of finding a way together out of the ordinary. 

   The Nachimbong (나침봉) is South Korean boy group Stray Kids' official lightstick. It was first

	revealed via Twitter on November 16, 2019 and it was officially released on November 23, 2019.
